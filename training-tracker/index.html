Kettlebell Bro Split

Weekly routine focused on muscle groups (2 Days Back)

Day 1: Chest & Core

  • KB Floor Press (3x10)
  • KB Push-ups (3xMax)
  • KB Pullovers (3x12)

Day 2: Back 1 (Heavy)

  • Heavy KB Rows (4x8/arm)
  • KB Deadlifts (4x10)
  • Renegade Rows (3x8/arm)

Day 3: Legs

  • Goblet Squats (4x12)
  • KB Romanian Deadlifts (4x10)
  • KB Walking Lunges (3x10/leg)

Day 4: Shoulders & Arms

  • KB Overhead Press (3x10/arm)
  • KB Halos (3x10/dir)
  • KB Curls & Overhead Tricep Ext (3x12)

Day 5: Back 2 (Explosive/Volume)

  • KB Swings (4x15-20)
  • KB High Pulls (3x12/arm)
  • KB Clean (3x8/arm)

Day 6 & 7: Rest / Cardio

Use these days for Easy Runs, stretching, or full rest to recover.
